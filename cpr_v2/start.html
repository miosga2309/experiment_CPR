Welcome!
Thank you for participating in our experiment. We appreciate your time. The experiment will take about 15 minutes and consists of two games. For each game, you will join a group and participate with other people in the experiment.

You can win cash prizes after you have completed the experiment as compensation for your time. Certain behavior during the games can increase your chances of winning. That behavior will be explained before each game. The prizes are:
  • 1 x 150 EUR
  • 2 x 50 EUR
All information provided by you will be treated confidentially, will be anonymised and used for scientific purposes only. You can stop participating whenever you like. Your participation is highly appreciated.

The study is part of the master thesis of Jonas Miosga (jonas.miosga@uni-konstanz.de) and is supervised by Prof. Ulf-Dietrich Reips. In case of any questions, please contact Jonas Miosga.
