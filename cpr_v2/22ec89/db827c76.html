Final game

You will participate in the next and final game with one other player. Please enter a username and click the 'Log-on!'-Button as soon as you are ready. Finding a group usually goes quickly because the players from the first group play as well.

Both you and the other person will be making choices by choosing one of three options on the next pages. Your own choices will produce points for both yourself and the other person. Likewise, the other's choice will produce points for him/her and for you. Both the points that you give to yourself and the points you receive from the other can increase your chances of winning a prize. The more points you receive, the better for you, and the more points the other receives, the better for him/her.

Have in mind that there are no right or wrong answers.
Collecting participants



A button will appear when the group is full. Please click it when you are ready. When everybody is ready, the experiment will start.
