Imagine you have to determine how much energy in kilowatt hours you will use for heating your home. Using more energy will result in a higher comfort level, in this context expressed through a hypothetical monetary reward that can increase your chances of winning the prize.

You receive the energy from a local source which you share with five other real persons. The energy source is mainly based on renewable energy (wind, solar), which is why the resupply of energy is not constant.

On the following pages, please choose an amount of energy. Be aware that you and your fellow participants should choose carefully so that the pool is not going to be exhausted.

For every decision, you will be informed about the current energy level in the pool and the amount the other group members consumed last round. The information will be displayed in two charts.
